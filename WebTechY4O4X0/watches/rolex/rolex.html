Rolex - Submariner

1956

The first Submariner was introduced to the public in 1954 at the Basel Watch Fair. It was the first watch to be waterproof up to 100 metres (330 ft). The Rolex Submariner is considered a classic among wristwatches, manufactured by one of the most widely recognized luxury brands in the world.

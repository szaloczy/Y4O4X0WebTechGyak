Cartier - Santos De Cartier

2022

This stylish Cartier comes from the Cartier Santos collection and has a steel gold strap in combination with a steel gold case that has a unique square shape. The dial of this model has a white dial with a Roman hour indication in combination with a date window at the 6 o'clock position. The model has an automatic 1847 MC caliber. The model has been tested for water resistance up to 100 meters.

Welcome to the World of Watches!

This page showcases the functionality of watches and highlights prominent brands such as Rolex, Orient, Cartier, and Baum.

The world of watches is exciting and diverse. Behind every timepiece lies a unique mechanism capable of precise timekeeping and stylish aesthetics.

Explore the table below to learn more about these watches and their operations!

Watches By Functionality
Analogue
Standout feature: Representation of the classic & traditional side of timepieces
  • Traditional clock face
  • Three-hand movement
  • For every budget
Digital
Standout feature: An electronic display
  • Time in numerical digits
  • Packed with features like a GPS, pedometer & more
  • For every budget
Chronograph
Standout feature: More complications & functions with subdials often include stopwatches, tachymeters & more
  • Powered solely by any light
  • Doesn't require battery replacements
  • Accurate to about +/- 15 seconds a month
  • Quartz crystal timekeeping
Hybrid
Standout feature: Combines digital features with traditional watch mechanics
  • Analogue & digital combo
  • Similar smart watch functionality
  • For every budget
Explore the elegant world of wristwatches, where craftsmanship meets sophistication in every detail.
Watches By Movement
Automatic
Standout feature: Self-winding via kinetic energy.
  • Automatic watches store energy automatically by utilising the movement of your wrist
  • Accurate to about +/- 20 seconds a day
  • Available for all budgets
Mechanikal
Standout feature: intricate series of tiny components work together to power the timepiece
  • Mechanical movements never require batteries because they require periodical hand-winding to operate
  • AAccurate to about +/-20 seconds a day
  • Super reliable and just plain cool to look at
  • Higher price tag than other movements
Solar
Standout feature: The abality to convert light energy into electronic energy
  • Powered solely by any light
  • Doesn't require battery replacements
  • Accurate to about +/- 15 seconds a month
  • Quartz crystal timekeeping
Quartz
Standout feature: Very accurate and require minimal maintenance
  • The most affordable watch movement
  • Uses electronic circuits regulated by quartz crystal
  • Requires a battery to function & battery replacement every 2-3 years
  • Accurate to about +/- 15 seconds a month
